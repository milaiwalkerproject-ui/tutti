## Full version (for an About / Terms page)

**Tutti is a discovery tool, not the official source.**

We help you find local orchestras, ensembles, and auditions and see season, pricing, and audition information at a glance. We then route you to each organization's own website, which is always the authoritative source.

**Please verify before you rely on anything.** Listings on Tutti may be incomplete, out of date, or — where clearly marked — illustrative sample data. Tuition, ticket prices, concert dates, audition windows, age requirements, and application steps can change without notice. Before making a decision, registering, or paying anything, confirm the details directly with the organization through its official website.

**We don't collect or submit applications.** Tutti never takes registrations, payments, or audition sign-ups on an organization's behalf. When you tap "Visit website" or an audition link, you leave Tutti and deal directly with that organization.

**Tell us if something's wrong.** If a listing looks inaccurate or outdated, let us know so we can review and correct it.

**No guarantee.** Tutti is provided as a convenience. We work to keep listings accurate but can't guarantee that every detail is current or error-free, and we aren't responsible for decisions made solely on the information shown here.
