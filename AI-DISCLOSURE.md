# Tutti — AI Disclosure & Diligence Statement

*This statement documents how AI was used in building Tutti. It concerns the development process. For how users should rely on the information Tutti surfaces, see `DATA-ACCURACY-NOTICE.md`.*

---

In developing Tutti — a map-based directory that helps people find local youth orchestras, ensembles, and auditions — I used Claude, an AI assistant developed by Anthropic, to support research, software development, data modeling, and documentation. Claude contributed to building the front-end application, structuring the underlying data schema and design system, and drafting supporting technical materials. All product direction, scope decisions, and final judgments were my own; the AI executed against my specifications rather than setting them.

I affirm that all AI-generated and co-created content underwent thorough review before release. I verified factual claims, tested application functionality, and confirmed that any sample or placeholder data is clearly labeled as illustrative and not presented as verified real-world information. Where the product surfaces organization details — pricing, schedules, audition windows, and similar — these are either sourced and verified or explicitly marked as prototype sample data not to be relied upon.

While AI assistance was instrumental to the development process, I maintain full responsibility for the product, including its accuracy, reliability, and fitness for its intended purpose. This disclosure is made in the spirit of transparency and to acknowledge the role of AI in the creation process.
