How Tutti works

The map in five steps — and how listings get checked.

Using the map

  1. Search a city or tap "Near me" to centre the map.
  2. Coloured pins are orchestras: brass = youth, garnet = professional, teal = community, slate = collegiate.
  3. Tap a pin or a card for season, ensembles by age, prices and auditions.
  4. "Compare" puts two or three organizations side by side.
  5. The Auditions tab gathers every open and upcoming audition, with links out.

How we check listings

Every listing is curated by a person and checked against the organization's own website — nothing is scraped or auto-generated. Confirmed details cite the source we checked; anything we couldn't confirm shows "Check their site," which takes you straight to the right page to verify it yourself.

Each organization's panel shows when its details were last checked. Things change — tuition, dates and audition windows can move without notice — so before you register or pay anything, confirm on the organization's own website. Their site is always the authority; Tutti is the map, not the destination.

We never take applications, payments or sign-ups. When you tap "Apply on their site" or "Visit website," you deal directly with the organization.

Spotted something wrong or out of date? Email us — a person reviews every report.

What Tutti costs

Nothing. Tutti is free for families and players — no account needed to browse, and organizations can't pay to change how they appear.

Built openly

Tutti is made by a professional musician and music teacher, with AI assistance under human review. A person checks — and is responsible for — every listing.

What sign-in stores

Signing in does one thing: it keeps your Saved list. Here's exactly what that involves.

You can use all of Tutti without an account. Signing in with Google or Apple is optional — it only exists so the organizations you save follow you across reloads and devices.

What we store

  • A basic account identifier from Google or Apple — an ID and the email address on that account.
  • The list of organizations you save (just their IDs).

What we don't store

  • No passwords (Google/Apple handle sign-in), no payment details, no information about your child.
  • No browsing or location history. We never sell or share your information.

You're in control

  • Sign out anytime. Removing a save deletes that entry from your account.
  • Tutti is a discovery layer — it never collects applications. “Apply” always hands you off to the organization's own site.
